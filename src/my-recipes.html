Mozzarella Sticks

Description:

Fried, cheesy, cheese sticks.

Information:

  • Prep time: 15 min
  • Cook time: 15 min
  • Total time: 30 min
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying
  • 16 ounce package mozzarella cheese sticks

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mix the eggs and water.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs and garlic salt in a medium bowl. In a medium bowl, blend the flour and cornstarch.
  3. In a large heavy saucepan, heat the oil to 365 degrees F.
  4. Coat each mozzarella stick in the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, then in the bread crumbs and finally into the oil. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.

Chicken Alfredo

Description:

Fettucine noodles with chicken, alfredo sauce, and parsley.

Information:

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 18 min
  • Total Time: 33 min
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 16 oz penne pasta (cooked)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Directions

  1. In a pan, on medium heat, melt butter and add breast.
  2. Season w/ salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Cook until chicken is ready. Set on side.
  3. In same pan, in medium heat, add butter and garlic. Cook till garlic is soft.
  4. Add half of the flour to the garlic and butter, stirring until incorporated. Then add the rest of the flour and stir.
  5. Pour in the milk a little bit at a time, stirring well in between, until fully incorporated and sauce begins to thicken.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil, and stir well to incorporate.
  7. Add parmesan cheese and stir until melted.
  8. Pour the sauce over cooked penne pasta, add the chicken and mix well.
  9. Add parsley and extra parmesan. Mix well.

Fudgy Brownies

Description:

Moist, Chocolaty, Fudgy, Warm Brownies that melt in your mouth.

Information:

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 30 min
  • Total Time: 45 min
  • Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. high quality, semi-sweet, chocolate
  • 12 tablespoos of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanila extract
  • 3/4 cup of all purpose flower
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Directions

  2. Chop the chocolate into chunks. Melt half of the chocolate in the microwave for 20 seconds.
